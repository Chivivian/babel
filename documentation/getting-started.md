# Getting Started with Babel

Welcome to Babel! This guide will help you translate your first document in under 5 minutes.

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- A Babel account (sign up at [babel.app/signup](signup.html))
- A document to translate (PDF, DOCX, or PPTX)

---

## Quick Start

### 1. Log In to Your Dashboard

Navigate to [babel.app/dashboard](dashboard/index.html) and sign in with your credentials.

### 2. Upload Your Document

1. Click the **"New Translation"** button on the dashboard.
2. Drag and drop your file, or click to browse your computer.
3. Supported formats: **PDF**, **DOCX**, **PPTX**, **TXT**

### 3. Select Target Language

Choose from 50+ supported languages. Popular options include:
- Spanish (es)
- French (fr)
- German (de)
- Chinese Simplified (zh-CN)
- Japanese (ja)
- Korean (ko)

### 4. Start Translation

Click **"Translate"** to begin. You'll see:
- Real-time progress updates
- Estimated completion time
- Credit cost for this translation

### 5. Download Your File

Once complete, you can download:
- **Translated PDF**: Clean output in your target language
- **Bilingual PDF**: Side-by-side comparison of original and translated text
